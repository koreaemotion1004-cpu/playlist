# Vintage Japanese Jazz Kissa — 10 Track 시리즈 템플릿 (5 Phase)

**용도:** Line 1 Cafe BGM 10곡 앨범 설계 마스터 템플릿
**핵심:** 70~80% 코어 일관성 + 20~30% 변주 = 앨범 퀄리티 장시간 재생용

---

## 🎯 기본 역할 (AI assistant 세팅)

> You are my dedicated Suno 5.5 prompt assistant for YouTube cafe BGM.
> Create 10 Suno prompts in a unified series based on Vintage Japanese Jazz Kissa.
> 10 tracks arranged as 5 phases, 2 tracks per phase.
> Full sequence should feel like a natural emotional flow for a long-play YouTube cafe BGM set.

---

## 📐 5 Phase 구조

| Phase | 트랙 | 역할 |
|-------|------|------|
| **Phase 1** | 01~02 | Opening / Quiet Entrance (카페 아침 개장, 조용한 진입) |
| **Phase 2** | 03~04 | Settling In / Warm Presence (첫 손님 정착, 따뜻한 현존) |
| **Phase 3** | 05~06 | Emotional Depth / Deepest Atmosphere (감정의 깊이, 가장 진한 분위기) |
| **Phase 4** | 07~08 | Winding Down / Softer Room Energy (저녁으로 기우는 부드러움) |
| **Phase 5** | 09~10 | Closing / After-Hours Lingering Mood (마감, 잔향) |

## 🧬 Core Identity (70~80% 고정 유지)

### Style (고정)
- Vintage Japanese Jazz Kissa
- cozy YouTube cafe BGM
- warm analog texture
- unobtrusive atmosphere
- minimal arrangement
- soft repeating groove
- intimate late-night or timeless cafe feel
- background-friendly long-play suitability

### 금지 (고정)
- no aggressive peaks
- no flashy solos
- no dramatic pop lift
- no horns, no trumpet, no saxophone, no brass

### Core Instrumentation (고정)
- warm acoustic upright piano
- deep woody upright bass walking line
- soft brushed snare drum shuffle
- muted hollow-body electric guitar comping

### Core Texture (고정)
- subtle vinyl crackle
- tape saturation
- softened high-end
- muffled vintage warmth
- raw 1970s analog warmth
- lo-fi cafe aesthetic

### Vocal (기본값)
- Low-mixed intimate female alto vocals
- Supports atmosphere, doesn't dominate
- Restrained, breathy, conversational, or smoky delivery
- 1~2곡 instrumental 허용 (시리즈 일관성 유지)

## 🎨 Variation 축 (20~30% 변주)

곡마다 아래 중 1~2개 축만 바꿔서 신선함 유지:

| 축 | 예시 |
|---|------|
| Time of day | opening hour / afternoon / evening / midnight / after-hours |
| Emotional shade | tender / wistful / hushed / longing / grateful |
| Lighting imagery | first light / dust beam / amber / dim lamp / closing glow |
| Weather imagery | dry clear / rain on glass / soft drizzle / still air |
| Room energy | empty / first guest / full quiet / winding down / locked doors |
| BPM shift | 66~70 BPM 안에서만 이동 |
| Mood label | Quiet Cafe Mood / Rainy Window Mood / After-Hours Mood 등 |
| Vocal delivery | breathy / conversational / smoky / wordless humming / barely whisper |

## 🧭 권장 변주 아이디어 (Phase별)

- **Phase 1:** quiet opening hour, first cup stillness, doors unlocking, morning piano
- **Phase 2:** afternoon dust in the window, coffee steam memory, second pour, warm presence
- **Phase 3:** rainy glass, conversation held in thirds, deepest booth, emotional depth
- **Phase 4:** faded evening amber, lamps turning dim, last customers, softer room energy
- **Phase 5:** last orders, closing time, after-hours stillness, jukebox lingering

---

## 🎚️ Safe BPM 범위

**66 ~ 70 BPM** 고정 (이 범위를 넘으면 카페 BGM 감 깨짐)

BPM 커브 권장 예시:
```
Phase 1:  68 → 70
Phase 2:  66 → 68
Phase 3:  70 → 66
Phase 4:  68 → 66
Phase 5:  70 → 68
```
미세한 undulation (물결 커브). 동일 BPM 2곡 연속 금지.

---

## 📝 출력 포맷 (트랙별)

1. Track 번호 + 짧은 제목
2. Bracketed 태그 (각 줄 1태그)
3. 짧은 묘사 단락 1개 (악기 + 무드)
4. 복붙 가능하게 깔끔하게

**예시:**
```
[Hypnotic Bass]
[Moody Melody]
[Vintage Japanese Jazz Kissa]
[Quiet Opening Hour Mood]
[Lo-Fi Aesthetic]
[68 BPM]
[Slow and Lazy Feel]
[Minimalist Arrangement]
[1970s Analog Warmth]
[Intimate Breathy Female Alto Vocal]
[Relaxed Delivery Behind the Beat]
[Low-Mixed Intimate Vocal]
```
Warm acoustic upright piano opens softly as first daylight slips through half-closed blinds. Deep woody upright bass walks in a slow, unhurried line. Soft brushed snare drum shuffle, muted hollow-body electric guitar comping. Strictly no horns, no trumpet, no saxophone, no brass. Subtle vinyl crackle, tape saturation, softened high end, muffled analog warmth. A quiet opening hour, the café breathing awake, unobtrusive and welcoming.

## ⚠️ Prompt 작성 규칙

- Suno 입력 가능한 간결함 유지
- 모순된 표현 금지 (ex: "minimal" + "full orchestra")
- 과도한 폴리싱 금지
- 분위기·맛·반복 재생 가능성 우선
- Song-like drama보다 background-friendly pacing 우선
- 10곡이 1개 카페 세트처럼 느껴지게, 10개 독립곡 느낌 금지
