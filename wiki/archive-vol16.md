## Style of Music (10곡 모두 동일 · 971자)

```
[Hypnotic Bass]
[Mesmerizing Melody]
[Vintage Japanese Jazz Kissa]
[74 BPM]
[Intimate Breathy Female Alto Vocal]
[Energy Decreases Over Time]
[No Dramatic Build]
[No Intro Humming]
[Instrumental Jazz Intro 8 Bars]

Short instrumental jazz intro: warm piano alone for 4 bars, soft brushed drums settle in at bar 4, very soft muted guitar at bar 6, no humming or vocalizing during intro, lyrics enter at bar 8. Soft 1940s cafe swing, background-friendly. Warm piano main instrument. Very soft muted guitar as subtle texture. Female vocal calm, intimate, breathy. Minimal percussion, rhythm carried by vocal phrasing and piano chords. Energy gradually decreases, vocals soften, ending calmer than start. Avoid energetic or expressive delivery. Subtle vinyl crackle, tape saturation.

No saxophone, trumpet, horns, brass. No jazz trio, no walking bass, no strong rhythm section. No dramatic build or climax. No modern production, no pop hooks. No "Mmm" or "Oh" humming intro.
```
